Introduces an eVoucher system on phones

- Receive eVouchers from issuer to beneficiary
- Transfer eVouchers from device to device (C2C)
-- Using NFC with confirmation
-- SMS
-- RFID
- Payment: Transfering voucher from device to payment terminal (C2B)
- Clearance between business and issuer over a network
- Check balance, expiration, history etc from phone

Very interesting design, very low level and somewhat old, based on MIDlets (java) infrastructure.

speed of cryptographic operations were a major concern
